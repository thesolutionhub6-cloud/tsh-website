Eligibility Requirements

To qualify for most Canadian economic immigration programs, you will generally need the following:

  • Valid passport with at least 6 months remaining validity
  • Language test results meeting a minimum of CLB 5 (IELTS, CELPIP, or PTE Core)
  • Educational Credential Assessment (ECA) from a designated organization such as WES
  • Work experience in an eligible NOC code (minimum 1 year continuous)
  • Clean background check (no criminal inadmissibility)

Eligibility varies by program. Contact us for a personalized assessment based on your specific situation.
